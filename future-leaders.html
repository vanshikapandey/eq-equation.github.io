Future leaders: This is for your young adult wants to get a jump start to its potential

Being a kid is tough, being a parent is tougher.

Young Adults will be our future politicians, doctors, engineers, innovators, scientists, activists, construction workers, artists, and much more.

The education system across the globe focuses on hard skills (IQ) to equip them to land admission into a top university or enter a job. However, there is minimal direction on the way forward with survival techniques. Developing soft skills early in life saves decoding time in the future and sets them on the fast track towards their dreams or to better communicate their needs to their caregivers.

At EQ Equation, we create a safe place for adolescents to

  • Build non-cognitive skills like motivation, self-control, self-discovery
  • Effective communication
  • Verbalizing and process thoughts
  • Growth mindset
